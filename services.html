Marwari Mehndi Design

Marwari mehndi is an authentic form of traditional mehndi which comes from the culturally rich state of Rajasthan. The intricate, thin strokes that define Marwari mehndi form traditional motifs and patterns like horses, Dulha-Dulhan, palanquins, flora and fauna and other such delicate, yet royal patterns. Bridal mehndi designs looks beautiful but marwari bridal mehndi designs are exceptional. This design consists of peacocks, mangoes, flowers and net beads. The specialty of these designs are it covers the whole hand, from finger tips to elbow.
